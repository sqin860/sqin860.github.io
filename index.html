About

Si Qin is a Principal Researcher and Research Manager in the Data, Knowledge, and Intelligence (DKI) area of Microsoft AI. Since joining Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) in November 2018, he has been dedicated to advancing AI for IT Operations (AIOps), focusing on leveraging signal processing, machine learning, and optimization technologies to develop high-quality cloud services. In June 2024, Si Qin joined Microsoft AI, where his research and applied science efforts have centered on developing large language model (LLM)-based retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems and agent frameworks. These include the widely recognized open-source projects Taskweaver and UFO.

At Microsoft, Si Qin has earned notable recognition, including the Distinguished Contribution Award at the 2021 Microsoft Machine Learning, AI, and Data Science (MLADS) Conference. The research technologies have been successfully transferred into multiple Microsoft product divisions, such as Microsoft Azure, Office 365, Bing, and SharePoint. His team has also been honored with the Best Tech Transfers of the Year Award at MSRA and the Team Award at Microsoft Asia-Pacific R&D.

Currently a Senior Member of IEEE, Si Qin conducted research in array signal processing and received his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Villanova University in 2016 under the guidance of Prof. Yimin D. Zhang and Prof. Moeness G. Amin. Before joining MSRA, he worked on speech signal processing at Sogou and Didi between 2016 and 2018. Si Qin's academic achievements include several prestigious awards, such as the Best Student Paper Award at the 2012 IEEE International Conference on Microwave and Millimeter Wave Technology (ICMMT), the First Prize in the Student Paper Competition at the 2014 IEEE Benjamin Franklin Symposium on Microwave and Antenna Sub-systems (BenMAS), the 2018 Young Author Best Paper Award from the IEEE Signal Processing Society, and the 2021 Best Paper Award from the European Association for Signal Processing (EURASIP).

秦思是微软数据、知识与智能（DKI）组的首席研究经理。自2018年11月加入微软亚洲研究以来，他始终致力于推动AI在智能运维（AIOps）中的创新应用，专注于通过信号处理、机器学习和优化技术开发高质量的云服务。 2024年6月，秦思加入微软人工智能团队，进一步将研究重点聚焦于基于大语言模型（LLM）的检索增强生成（RAG）系统和智能体框架的研发工作。他所在团队开发的开源项目 Taskweaver 和 UFO 在业内享有广泛认可，并取得了显著成果。 在微软，秦思荣获了2021年微软机器学习、人工智能与数据科学大会（MLADS）"杰出贡献奖"。他研发的技术已成功转化并应用于多个微软产品部门，包括 Microsoft Azure、Office 365、Bing 和 SharePoint。他和所在团队因此获得了"微软亚洲研究院年度最佳技术转化奖"以及"微软亚太研发团队奖"。

秦思目前是IEEE高级会员，他曾于2016年在维拉诺瓦大学（Villanova University）获得电气工程博士学位，师从Yimin D. Zhang教授和 Moeness G. Amin 教授。在加入微软亚洲研究院之前，他于2016年至2018年间在搜狗和滴滴从事语音信号处理研究。 秦思曾多次获得国际学术奖，包括：2012年IEEE国际微波与毫米波技术会议（ICMMT）"最佳学生论文奖"，2014年IEEE本杰明·富兰克林微波与天线子系统研讨会（BenMAS）"学生论文竞赛一等奖"，2018年IEEE信号处理学会"青年作者最佳论文奖"，以及2021年欧洲信号处理协会（EURASIP）"最佳论文奖"。
